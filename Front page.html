Police Escape - the game

  • Your objective is to use your geographical knowledge to make a successful escape journey through Europe.
  • You play as a thief starting your journey in Helsinki, Finland.
  • You will make five flights, with the number of escape options declining each round.
  • Choose your destinations wisely — the police are always waiting for you at the nearest airport.
  • You win the game if you complete the entire journey without getting caught.
