Are you ready to outsmart the police on a flight adventure across Europe?

Your objective in Police Escape is to use your geographical knowledge in order to make a successful escape journey through Europe. In the role of a thief, you will start your journey in Helsinki, Finland and make five flights with the number of escape options declining for each round. Be sure to make the right choice, since the police are always waiting for you at the nearest airport. You have completed the game successfully if you are able to make the whole journey without getting caught.
